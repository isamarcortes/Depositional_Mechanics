Our coordinates system usual convention

For directions we use

  • $x$ will be the downslope / down system direction
  • $y$ will be cross-stream direction
  • $z$ will be normal to $x$ and $y$ in a dominatly vertical direction

For fluid velocitites we will use $U=(u, v, w)$ as coordinates. For sediment velocititeswe will use $U_s=(u_s, v_s, w_s)$
